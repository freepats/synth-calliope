Synth Lead Calliope
-------------------

Version 2020-05-12

General MIDI instrument #83: Synth Lead 3 (calliope).

Made by roberto@zenvoid.org as the General MIDI instrument #83 for the FreePats project, using a combination of Hexter and ZynAddSubFX / Yoshimi software synthesizers.
